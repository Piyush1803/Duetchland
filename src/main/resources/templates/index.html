What is the Ausbildung Program?

The Ausbildung Program is a vocational training program in Germany that offers students and young professionals a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience while studying. It is a dual education system, combining theoretical education at a vocational school and practical training at a company. This program is highly respected and provides a direct pathway to employment in Germany.
